CLM Misting Solutions offers support to DIY'ers and professional installers to help with the installation of our system. Our goal is to provide a best-in-class experience and a system that protects outdoor spaces from mosquitoes and flies. Whether for residential backyards, horse barns, or outdoor dining areas, our expert team ensures full coverage, long-term reliability, and optimal system performance.

    • Wall mount the misting system to the structure or tank mount if wall mounting is not optimal.
