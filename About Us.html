Who is CLM Misting Solutions?

CLM Misting Solutions specializes in advanced mosquito and fly control systems designed for residential, commercial, and equestrian spaces. Our misting technology provides an environmentally friendly, reliable, and effective solution to eliminate pests. Whether for a backyard, barn, or restaurant patio, our customized solutions offer long-term protection.

  • Timed misting cycles are pre-set to spray 2-3 times per day (adjustable).
  • Nozzles release an ultra-fine spray to eliminate mosquitoes and flies on contact.
  • Nozzles are strategically placed along fences, patios, barns, or other structures for targeted coverage.
  • Smart control options include manual remote activation for extra sprays as needed.
  • Creates a residual protective barrier to keep outdoor spaces mosquito and fly free.
  • Timed treatments are more effective than once every three weeks or monthly treatments.
  • Hands-free protection - no need for hand sprayers or blowers.
  • Customizable for barns, residential backyards, restaurants, and event spaces.
  • Use only EPA-approved solutions designed for misting applications.
  • All-natural botanical options available.
  • No need to dose yourself with mosquito repellant.
  • Advanced digital system with ½ horsepower motor, 140 GPH pump, and control manifold which supports up to 90 nozzles at 200 PSI.
  • Weather-resistant design with durable polyurethane outer shell.
  • Tamper-proof lockable enclosure.
  • Customizable scheduling with a digital control panel.
  • Optional smart control for Wi-Fi-enabled misting management.
  • Eco-friendly solutions.
  • Environmentally friendly for children and pets when used properly with EPA-approved formulations.
  • Optimized misting schedule providing the right dose at the right time.
